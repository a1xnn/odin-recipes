Tamales

Mexico's best dish for any occasion!

Tamales are a traditional Mexican dish made with corns based dough mixture that is filled with various meats, wrapped in corn husk.

Ingredigents

  • Masa harina
  • Broth
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Lard
  • Dried corn husk

Instructions

  1. Place corn husks in a bowl of very hot water for 30 mintues or until softened
  2. Prepare the filling. You will need 4 cups of filling for one batch of tamale dough. For this recipe we will use red chili pork.
  3. In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to beat the lard and 2 tablespoons of broth until fluffy. Combine the masa flour, baking powder,salt and cumin in a medium bowl.
  4. Stir into the lard mixture and beat well with an electric mixer. Add broth until the dough becomes very soft. After cover the mixing with damp paper towel to keep from drying out.
  5. Assemble the tamales.
  6. Cook tamales in a steamer pop. Boil the water and once boiling, reduce to a simmer and steam for 1 to hours.
