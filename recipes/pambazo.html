Pambazo

A Mexican sandwich that you must try!

A sandwich made of a particular type of bread stuffed with potatoes and chorizo. With it being drenched in guajillo sauce. Can be served with lettuce, cheese and cream.

Ingredients

  • Diced potatoes
  • 3 guajillo peppers with seeds and veins removed
  • 2 chorizo
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 6 teleras
  • shredded lettuce
  • crumbled cheese
  • Mexican cream

Instructions

  1. Cook diced potatoes in a saucepan with hot water on medium-high heat. Cook for 10-12 mintues.
  2. While the potatoes are cooking place the guajillo peppers in a bowl with hot water to soften.
  3. Cook the chorizo in a skillet over medium-high heat for 8 mintues.
  4. Once the potatoes are ready drain and stir them into the skillet with the chorizo. Keep cooking for three mintues.
  5. In blender, put the guajillo peppers, 1 cup of water, garlic clove, and oregano. Blend until you have a smooth sauce and strain into a bowl.
  6. Heat a griddle and add a 1/2 tablespoon of oil. Place the teleras and brush over with the guajillo on each side until they are warm and crispy. Repeat the process with the rest of the rolls and added oil as needed.
  7. Assemble the pambazo by adding chorizo and potatoes into the sliced teleras. Top with the shredded lettuce, cream and cheese. Enjoy!
